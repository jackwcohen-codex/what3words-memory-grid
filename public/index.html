How to play

Three what3words squares will flash on the map, one after another.

Watch where each square appears and remember the address shown with it.

After the flashes, one of those addresses appears again in the right-hand panel. Click the square where you saw it.

Closer guesses score more points. On Beginner difficulty with Relaxed pace, an exact square is worth 100.

Higher difficulty levels and faster study pace multiply that score, so exact matches can be worth much more.

Use Difficulty to change the map area, and Study pace to change how long each square stays visible.
